Privacy Policy for Tic Tac Toe Glow: XO Puzzle

Effective Date: September 30, 2025

1. General Information

Meetanshi Technologies LLP built the Tic Tac Toe Glow: XO Puzzle game as a free entertainment application. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles user information.

  • The game is designed mainly as an offline puzzle game.
  • No account creation is required.
  • No personal or sensitive user data is collected, stored, or shared.
  • No unnecessary device permissions are requested.
  • No advertisements or third-party SDKs are integrated.

Our main goal is to provide a safe, fun, and distraction-free gaming experience.

2. Information We Collect

We want to make it absolutely clear:

  • We do not collect personal data (such as name, email, phone number, location, or contacts).
  • We do not collect device information or usage statistics.
  • We do not track user activity across apps or devices.
  • All game progress is stored locally on the device and is deleted automatically if the app is uninstalled.

3. Offline Gameplay

You can fully enjoy the game without an internet connection.

  • Single-player and local 2-player modes work completely offline.
  • No background services or online servers are required for normal play.

This ensures that your privacy remains fully protected.

4. No Advertising & No Third Parties

  • No banner ads, pop-ups, or interstitial ads are shown.
  • No third-party analytics, ad networks, or SDKs are used.
  • We do not share any user data with third parties.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages, including children. Since we do not collect or share any personal data, parents and guardians can feel confident about its safety.

6. Security of Your Information

Because the game does not collect or store user information, there is no risk of misuse. The app is designed for entertainment only. All data remains under the user’s full control.
